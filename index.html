About

Nico Carlier, a 23-year-old full-stack software engineer, pivoted from mechanical engineering to software after discovering his passion for programming at the University of Auckland. He furthered his skills at App Academy, San Francisco, mastering JavaScript, SQL, and Ruby, alongside frameworks like React and Ruby on Rails.


Post-bootcamp, Nico expanded his expertise into Python, Next.js, and TypeScript, engaging in projects, hackathons, and open-source contributions. He sharpened his problem-solving through over 100 LeetCode challenges and developed ‘Chess by Nico,’ showcasing rapid learning and implementation.


Professionally, he has contributed as a full-stack engineer at Couplr AI, enhancing platform integration with third-party services. He has recently joined Inventive AI, a dynamic YC startup in San Francisco, furthering his career in an agile, innovative environment.

Next.js & TypeScript Chess Platform
